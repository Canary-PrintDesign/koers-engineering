Koers & Associates

With a staff of 25, Koers & Associates provides customized and personal civil engineering services that specialize in municipal infrastructure and land development projects from central office locations in Parksville and the Comox Valley.

Our vision is to continue to solidify our market share on Vancouver Island, by concentrating on providing the best solutions for the engineering needs of our existing clients, with emphasis on environmental stewardship and sustainability.
