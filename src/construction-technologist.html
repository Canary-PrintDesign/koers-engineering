Join the team!

Wanted: Field Technologist

Koers & Associates Engineering Ltd. is looking to add technical depth to our company to support our expanding workload on Vancouver Island. We are currently seeking a Construction Technologist with a strong background in municipal infrastructure. Experience with inspection and survey layout would also be considered an asset.

The Position:

The successful candidate will generally be responsible for field review duties as well as contract administration support. Tasks will cover all aspects of the infrastructure construction cycle including:

• Construction inspection, including job reports and photo logs,
• Progress payment preparation and contract administration assistance,
• Contractor and client coordination,
• Material Testing coordination,
• Data collection and record drawing preparation.

The Candidate:

The candidate will have significant practical and progressive field experience. The position may also be suitable for a candidate looking to work on a part-time or contract basis. Preference will be given to those with the following:

• Registration as an AScT (an asset, but not required),
• Quantity measurement and contract administration experience,
• Possession of a valid BC Driver’s license,
• Topographic survey and construction layout experience with a robotic instrument,

What We Offer:

Koers & Associates Engineering Ltd. is a local, well-established, employee-owned civil engineering firm in Parksville BC. For over 35 years, Koers & Associates Ltd. has been providing consulting engineering services on a variety of civil infrastructure projects. Parksville is located 25 minutes north of Nanaimo on the east coast of sunny Vancouver Island where residents enjoy a pleasant climate all year round. We have deep ties and a strong presence in the area with loyal clients in the municipal, development and industrial communities. We can offer you:

• Competitive wages and benefits
• Flexible work schedule
• Support for professional development and growth
• Collaborative and supportive team and work environment
• Consistent workload of local projects requiring limited travel



If you are interested in a chance to work in a pleasant environment in the central Vancouver Island area with dedicated people on exciting and rewarding projects, then please submit your resume and a brief cover note by email to careers@koers-eng.com
